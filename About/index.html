About Me

I’m Daniel Schnieder, a Computer Engineering student at The Ohio State University with experience in Arduino, C++, and Raspberry Pi projects. I am currently the telemetry lead for Buckeye Solar Racing, where I develop systems for our solar car’s performance monitoring.

In addition to engineering, I am passionate about open-source development and teaching others through workshops and mentorship programs.
